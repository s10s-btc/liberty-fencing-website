Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Liberty Fencing ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Contact information: Name, phone number, and email address when you request a quote or use our fence designer tool.
  • Project information: Details about your fencing project, property address, and any fence design you create using our interactive tool.
  • Communications: Records of SMS messages, emails, or other communications between you and Liberty Fencing.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Prepare and deliver estimates for your fencing project
  • Schedule and coordinate fence installation services
  • Send you SMS messages and emails related to your estimate, project status, and appointment reminders
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send follow-up communications about your estimate (you may opt out at any time)

3. SMS Communications

By submitting a quote request or fence design through our website, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Liberty Fencing at the phone number you provide. These messages may include:

  • Estimate confirmations and details
  • Appointment scheduling and reminders
  • Project status updates
  • Follow-up messages about your estimate

To opt out of SMS messages at any time, reply STOP to any message we send. You may also reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your project status.

We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes. Your consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchasing any service.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Service providers: Companies that help us operate our business (e.g., SMS delivery via Twilio, email services), who are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and for a reasonable period afterward for business and legal purposes. You may request deletion of your personal information by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Opt out of email communications by clicking "unsubscribe" in any email

7. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
